Nature encompasses the entire physical world, from vast ecosystems and flowing rivers to microscopic organisms and cosmic phenomena, providing life-sustaining resources like food, water, and air, while its inherent beauty offers solace and balance to human well-being


A mountain is a large, elevated natural landform that rises significantly above the surrounding terrain, often with steep slopes and a peak


A lake is a natural or man-made basin on land filled with water, often formed by glacial melt or river activity, and surrounded by land
